Work Details

Description:

This virtual reality (VR) scene is a personal creation, a labor of love that I've crafted to immortalize a significant chapter in my life. It's a digital replica of the studio I lived in during my first year in Australia, a period that holds a special place in my heart. Each detail in this VR environment is meticulously designed by me, aiming to capture not just the physical layout of the studio, but also the emotions and experiences that were part of my everyday life there. The studio, recreated in this VR space, reflects the unique blend of the Australian lifestyle and my personal journey. From the sunlit windows that ushered in the bright Australian sun to the cozy corner where I spent countless hours reflecting and planning my future, every element is a tribute to those transformative days. The walls are adorned with digital art that echoes the vibrant culture I experienced in Australia, and the furniture layout mirrors the exact setup of my studio, offering a sense of familiarity and nostalgia.
